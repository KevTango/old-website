About me


I am currently a penultimate student studying towards a Bachelor of Electrical and Electronics Engineering (Honours) at the University of Auckland with an interest in the advancement of artificial intelligence and electronics. Fuelled by my curiosity to tinker with new technology to understand its capabilities, I am very eager to apply the knowledge learnt to stay in touch with the latest development in the technology sector.

Currently, I am working on my personal projects and am always up for a challenge.
